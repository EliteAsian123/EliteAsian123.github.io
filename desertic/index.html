About

Desertic is a voxel game that has strong inspiration from Minecraft. The point of the game is to survive in the desert you spawn in! Keep in mind that this game is in pre-alpha so it is not finished and there will be bugs.



Playing

To play Desertic, use to WASD keys to move and space to jump. If you are using a mouse, you can left click to place blocks and right click to place them. If not, use Q to break and E to place. Use the scroll wheel or 1 to 5 to scroll through items in your item bar. Press tab to open you inventory, you can move items by clicking on them. Use F11 to go in fullscreen mode. The game will save whenever you exit the game.
