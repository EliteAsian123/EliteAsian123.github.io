Warning! All browsers do not support this game!

This game requires some things that certain browsers do not support! If your browser doesn't support something, the game could be buggy or not work entirely. The recommended browser for this game is Google Chrome.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move
  • Space: Jump
  • Left Click or Q: Break
  • Right Click or E: Place
  • Scroll Wheel or 1-5: Change item
  • Tab: Inventory
