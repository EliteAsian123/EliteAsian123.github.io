Minecraft Reborn

About

Minecraft Reborn is a Minecraft Mod that aims to reimagine Minecraft. It is not in development yet, and development will start once Minecraft 1.15 comes out. In the mean time, I am working on the resource pack for this mod, which adds alternative block textures. The Resource Pack is still in alpha.
